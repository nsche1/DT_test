GUILD RULES & VALUES
  1. Don't be a jerk. This includes everyone we interact with, don't make Devious Tenants look bad in public. Treat everyone awesomely.


  2. Be tolerant of everyone. No hate speech or language aimed at being derogatory to a person's age, gender, sexuality, race, nationality or anything else. To accept all is to live gracefully.


  3. Include all in the fun of the game. Don't hate or exclude people for gearscores, ilevels, etc. No "HDPS only", epeen measuring, or other forms of elitism. Don't mandate that only those with certain builds, iLevels or items can participate. If someone meets the requirements the game sets to do content, then they have every right to do the content.


  4. No fighting, game bashing, whining, complaining, excessive profanity, politics, religion, etc in any chat. Keep chat clean. No one's going to jump down your throat for the occasional slipup. Please know the difference between a discussion and an argument. Please take arguments to private channels. If you can't deal with it, get ahold of an officer.


  5. No cheating or exploiting. Play the game as intended. If you are caught doing either you will be gkicked.


  6. Registration on the Website and an Intro Post in the "A bit about You!" forum thread is recommended, after that if you want to contribute to the community by using the Forums and website please feel free to do so. But You are not required to use the forums after you've registered and put up your intro post. We just want to get to know you and for you to know your fellow guildmates. Registration is also the only way to get promoted to Bryti.


  7. Last but not Least, No Drama, Devious Tenants is a Drama Free guild, so if it looks like it might cause Drama, don't do it.


These rules and values are here to make Devious Tenants a better place to hang your weapons. If for some reason you find that they don't fit you, or you're not interested in abiding by them, please move on and don't waste the time of the officers and others that are part of the guild.

Thanks,
Lufeng
